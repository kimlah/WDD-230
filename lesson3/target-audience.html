No matter the weather, we've got you covered.

Target Audience

Who: Residents around Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven, Idaho or visitors to the area.

Age: 14 to 100 years old.

Motivation: To improve resident enjoyment by supplying the weather knowledge needed to help them be prepared, no matter the weather.

Two Personas

Gemma Hanson

Demographics: 18 year old high school senior living in Soda Springs, Idaho. She likes to be prepared and not bring unneccesary coats or layers to school.

Goals and motivation for using this site: Be prepared but not overpacked for school. She doesn't want to bring her rain jacket unless there is a chance of rain but also doesn't want to wear jeans if it's going to be 90 degrees. She also wants to make plans with her friends for the next weekend and doesn't want to change plans at the last minute.

Technology: Always has her smart phone readily available. Uses a laptop daily for homework and social media.

Thomas O'Connor

Demographics: 42 year old, 2nd grade teacher. Has a Master's degree in Early Childhood Education. Married. Parent to 3 boys and 1 girl, ages 6 to 14.

Goals and motivation for using this site: Passionate about weather and the effects it has on the earth. Excited to share his passion with his class. He likes to hike and wants to check the weather in advance so he is prepared on hikes, especially with his kids.

Technology: Avid Apple user. Has a MacBook Air, current iphone, and an ipad. Uses Window products at work.

Senarios

  • What is the weather like today in Soda Springs, Idaho?
  • Are any storm fronts approaching SE Idaho?
  • What is the average rainfall this year in SE Idaho?
