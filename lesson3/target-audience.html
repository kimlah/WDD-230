Target Audience

Who: Elementary aged students, their parents or guardians and elementary educators.

Age: 5 to 11 year old students, 25-60 year old parents, guardians and educators.

Motivation: To improve weather unit study in elementary schools, bridging the gap between home and school learning.

Two Personas

Gemma Hanson

Demographics: 8 year old, 2nd Grade, student in Mr. O'Connor's class. Lives with her mom, two sisters and brother.

Goals and motivation for using this site: Learn about weather in SE Idaho with her 2nd grade class while completing a home based weather project with the help of her parents. She is excited to learn about storms and why they happen.

Technology: Has a ChromeBook from the school because her family does not have a computer. Her mom has a smart phone that they can access the website on as well.

Thomas O'Connor

Demographics: 42 year old, 2nd grade teacher. Has a Master's degree in Early Childhood Education. Married. Parent to 3 boys and 1 girl, ages 6 to 14.

Goals and motivation for using this site: Passionate about weather and the effects it has on the earth. Excited to share his passion with his student's while trying to connect and have participation with parents and guardians.

Technology: Avid Apple user. Has a MacBook Air, current iphone, and an ipad. Uses Window products at work.

Senarios

  • What is the weather like today in Soda Springs, Idaho?
  • Are any storm fronts approaching SE Idaho?
  • What is the average rainfall this year in SE Idaho?
  • What is the average temperature in Preston, Idaho in May? August?
  • What is the expected weather next week in Fish Haven, Idaho?
