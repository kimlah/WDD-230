Header, Headings, Footer

Inria Serif Regular 400 was chosen for the header, headings, and footer. It is a bolder font and easy to read in many font sizes. It is also not overwhelming and will not draw attention away from the purpose of the site.

Main Text

Montserrat Regular 400 was chosen for the main text of the site. It is an easy to read font that is not spaced too closely and is not heavily weighted.
