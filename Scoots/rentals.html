Rental Information

Vehicles can be reserved online or over the phone at least 24 hours in advance to receive the reservation price.

A half day rental is 3 hours of rental time.

No one under the age of 12 is allowed to ride on the scooters.

  • Unlimited mileage.
  • Liability Insurance is free of charge.
  • Fuel charge is not included in the rental price.
  • A $50 security deposit is required to confirm reservations.
  • A valid drivers license is required, in hand, at time of rental pickup.
  • Free delivery to Cozumel Hotel, Airbnb, or private residence for 2+ day rentals.
  • Payment via Google Pay, cash, credit card, debit card or PayPal.
  • Rentals can be returned to either location.
