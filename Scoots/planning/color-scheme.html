Color Scheme

Ming (#16697A) will be used as more of a background and accent color, probably as the background in the header and footer or nav menu.
Pacific Blue (#489FB5) paired with Middle Blue, the two colors will be the main color focus to reflect the ocean and the blue skies.
Middle Blue (#82C0CC) paired with Pacific Blue, the two colors will be the main color focus to reflect the ocean and the blue skies
Isabelline (#EDE7E3) is a nice neutral that almost reminds me of light colored sand, it's warm without bringing in other colors.
Orange Peel (#FFA21F) will be a pop of color when needed to break up the blues and add warmth.
